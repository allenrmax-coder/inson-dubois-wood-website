Atelier

A patient, uncompromising practice.

Architect. Designer. Builder. Principal since 2005.

Inson holds a Master of Architecture from Harvard's Graduate School of Design — where he studied under Frank Gehry, Rem Koolhaas, Wolf D. Prix and Zaha Hadid — and a Bachelor of Architecture from Cornell, graduating top of his class.

Before founding the studio in 2005, he served as design director for Juan Pablo Molyneux on the residential ocean liner Residensea, and director of design for David Easton, overseeing eighteen large-scale projects.

The atelier composes residences across the United States, Sweden, Italy, France, Austria, Japan and Thailand — guided in equal measure by classical training and quiet Buddhist principle, respecting and celebrating the spirit of every home.
